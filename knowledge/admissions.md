# Jessup University Admissions (Fictional Demo Data)

> This file contains fictional sample information for prototyping Alyssa.

## Undergraduate Admissions
- Recommended minimum high school GPA: 3.0
- Transfer applicant minimum GPA: 2.75
- Recommended prerequisites: Algebra II, pre-calculus, one programming or STEM elective
- Standardized tests: SAT/ACT optional
- Application steps:
  - Complete online application
  - Upload transcripts
  - Submit short statement (300-500 words)
  - Optional recommendation letter
- Deadlines:
  - Fall priority: March 1
  - Fall final: July 15
  - Spring final: December 1

## Graduate Admissions (MSCS - if enrolling)
- Minimum degree requirement: Bachelor's degree in CS or related field
- Recommended background: Data structures, algorithms, discrete math, linear algebra
- Required documents: Statement of purpose, resume, two recommendations, unofficial transcripts
- Testing: GRE optional; English proficiency required for non-native speakers
- Deadlines:
  - Fall entry: June 1
  - Spring entry: November 1

## International Student Admissions
- Required documents: Passport copy, financial affidavit, bank statement, credential evaluation as needed
- English proficiency:
  - TOEFL iBT 79
  - IELTS 6.5
  - Duolingo 105
- I-20 timeline: Typically issued within 10 business days after admission deposit and document approval
- International student support contact: international@jessup.demo.edu
