- CS 101 Intro to Programming (4)

- CS 220 Computer Organization (3)
- CS 230 Algorithms I (3)
- CS 240 Database Systems (3)
- CS 310 Operating Systems (3)
- CS 320 Software Engineering (3)
- CS 330 Computer Networks (3)
- CS 490 Senior Project I (3)
- CS 492 Senior Project II (3)

## Electives and Specializations
- Students complete at least 12 elective credits (4 courses)
- Available tracks:
  - Artificial Intelligence and Data
  - Cybersecurity
  - Full-Stack Development
  - Cloud and DevOps
- At least 6 elective credits must be 400-level

## Prerequisites and Co-requisites
- CS 201 requires CS 102
- CS 230 requires CS 201 and MATH 250
- CS 310 requires CS 220 and CS 230
- CS 490 requires senior standing and CS 320
- Prerequisite overrides require advisor approval and department chair sign-off

## Course Registration Guidance
- Recommended load:
  - Full-time undergraduate: 12-16 credits
  - High-intensity term for CS majors: up to 18 with advisor approval
- Add/drop windows:
  - Add by end of week 2
  - Drop without W by end of week 3
  - Withdraw with W by end of week 10
- Waitlist moves processed twice daily

## Transfer and Equivalency
- Up to 70 transfer credits accepted (lower-division limit applies)
- Transfer CS courses require syllabus review
- Typical minimum transfer grade for major courses: C
- Equivalency petitions reviewed in 7-10 business days
