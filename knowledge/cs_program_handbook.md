# Jessup University Computer Science Program Handbook (Fictional Demo Data)

> This file contains fictional sample information for prototyping Alyssa.

## Program Overview
- Degree name: Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS)
- Department: School of Natural and Applied Sciences, Computer Science Division
- Program mission: Prepare students to build ethical, industry-ready software with strong foundations in theory, systems, and teamwork.

## Admission Requirements
- First-year minimum GPA (recommended): 3.0 high school cumulative
- Transfer minimum college GPA: 2.75
- Recommended high school preparation: Algebra II, pre-calculus, one lab science
- Application process: Submit online application, transcript(s), short personal statement, and optional SAT/ACT
- Deadlines:
  - Fall priority: March 1
  - Fall final: July 15
  - Spring final: December 1

## Degree Requirements
- Total credits required: 120
- Core CS credits: 51
- Math/science credits: 24
- General education and electives: 45
- Minimum upper-division CS units at Jessup: 18

## Recommended Course Sequence
### Year 1
- Fall: CS 101 Intro to Programming, MATH 151 Calculus I, GE Writing I, GE Foundations
- Spring: CS 102 Object-Oriented Programming, MATH 152 Calculus II, GE Writing II, GE Humanities

### Year 2
- Fall: CS 201 Data Structures, CS 220 Computer Organization, MATH 250 Discrete Math, GE Social Science
- Spring: CS 230 Algorithms I, CS 240 Database Systems, MATH 260 Linear Algebra, GE Science

### Year 3
- Fall: CS 310 Operating Systems, CS 320 Software Engineering, CS elective, GE elective
- Spring: CS 330 Computer Networks, CS 340 Web Application Development, CS elective, GE elective

### Year 4
- Fall: CS 410 AI Fundamentals, CS 490 Senior Project I, CS elective, free elective
- Spring: CS 492 Senior Project II, CS 4xx elective, internship or practicum elective, free elective

## Academic Policies
- Minimum passing grade in CS major courses: C-
- Minimum cumulative GPA for graduation: 2.0
- Minimum major GPA for graduation: 2.3
- Academic probation: Cumulative GPA below 2.0 for a term places student on probation
- Dismissal review: Two consecutive probation terms may result in academic dismissal review
- Retake policy: A course may be repeated twice; highest grade counts in major GPA
- Transfer credit policy: Up to 70 lower-division transfer credits accepted; major equivalency approval required

## Registration and Enrollment
- Advising hold process: Students must meet with a CS advisor each term before registration opens
- Registration windows:
  - Seniors: starts Nov 1 / Apr 1
  - Juniors: starts Nov 3 / Apr 3
  - Sophomores: starts Nov 6 / Apr 6
  - First-year students: starts Nov 8 / Apr 8
- Waitlist policy: Auto-enroll runs twice daily when seats open and prerequisites are met
- Late add/drop deadlines:
  - Add deadline: end of week 2
  - Drop without W: end of week 3
  - Withdraw with W: end of week 10

## Internship / Capstone
- Internship required: Optional but strongly recommended
- Capstone required: Yes (CS 490 + CS 492)
- Capstone eligibility: Senior standing, CS 320 completed, at least 90 units earned
- Milestones:
  - Proposal due week 4 (CS 490)
  - Midpoint demo week 11 (CS 490)
  - Final demo and report week 13 (CS 492)

## Graduation Requirements
- Senior audit process: Complete degree audit with advisor one semester before final term
- Graduation application deadline:
  - Spring graduation: February 15
  - Fall graduation: September 20
- Residency requirement: Minimum 30 final credits completed at Jessup
