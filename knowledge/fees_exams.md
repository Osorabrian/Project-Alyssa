# Jessup University Fees and Exams (Fictional Demo Data)

> This file contains fictional sample information for prototyping Alyssa.

## Tuition and Academic Fees
- Undergraduate tuition (full-time): $23,400 per academic year
- Technology fee: $450 per year
- CS lab/course software fee: $300 per year
- Estimated books and materials: $1,100 per year
- New student enrollment deposit: $250 (applied to first term)

## Billing and Refund Policies
- Billing statements issued about 30 days before each term
- Payment due date: first day of classes each semester
- Late fee: $75 after grace period
- Refund schedule:
  - 100% tuition refund through day 7
  - 50% refund through day 14
  - 0% tuition refund after day 14

## Scholarships and Financial Aid
- Merit awards for incoming students based on GPA and holistic review
- CS innovation scholarship: up to $2,000/year (competitive)
- FAFSA code (demo): 001234
- Financial aid contact: financialaid@jessup.demo.edu

## Exam Policies
- Midterms generally during weeks 7-8
- Final exams during published finals week each term
- Make-up exams allowed for documented illness, emergency, or approved university activity
- Deferred final requests must be submitted within 48 hours of missed exam
- Approved accommodations coordinated through Student Accessibility Services

## Grading and Appeals
- Typical grade posting: within 7 business days after final exam
- Informal regrade request: within 5 business days of grade posting
- Formal grade appeal: within 15 business days after final grade publication
- Academic integrity violations follow Student Conduct process with instructor report and review panel
